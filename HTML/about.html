I have lived in Gilbert, Arizona for the majority of my life. Growing up there I was blessed to make and keep close friends as well as particpate in many school clubs and sports on top of receiving a great education.

I am the youngest child in my family and I have 3 incredible older siblings. My family members are the most important people in the world to me and I am honored to call my siblings and parents my best friends. Although this would be mortifying to some, I found that one of the greatest blessings I received during high school was attending the same school in which my mother taught.

I have many hobbies and interests that I try to nurture while seeking a higher education at BYU. Among them is a love of music. I have always sung in choirs and sought to improve my abilities by auditioing and participating in advanced choirs. I also enjoy playing the violin and piano. A great stress reliever for me is running. Coincidentally, I'm running more than ever with finals right around the corner! I am currently training for a half marathon and will be racing February 2022.
